About Crimson Scholars

Crimson Scholars is the best way to connect with amazing educators and learn online. We are an NYC company that believes that high quality tutoring, mentoring, and consulting should be accessible. We built our iPad app and web client from the ground up with simplicity and education in mind. Instead of struggling with a mouse, a wacom tablet, or a maze of dropdown menus, students can begin learning immediately, anywhere, anytime! Traffic jams and train delays are things of the past.

We also believe quality educators are key to effective learning, so we on,y work with tutors that have years of experience and success. They are dedicated to developing good study habits and making subjects less daunting for students. With our educators, we focus on interaction, personalization, and engagement.

Meet The Team

Hugh Heun Wong - Founder

Hugh began tutoring as a way to give back to his community. He worked with U.C. Berkeley's Academic Talent Development Program and the Coalinga Huron Avenal House to provide mathematics tutoring to disadvantaged students from the San Joaquin Valley. He then traveled to Shanghai and Hong Kong to teach English and the psychology of leadership. After receiving his Bachelors of Arts in Psychology and a citation in Mandarin from Harvard College, he worked in Boston's Chinatown, teaching dance and assisting fellow teachers. Afterwards, he lived in Seoul consulting and mentoring students through their high school and college admissions process.
